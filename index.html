Nika Sandler's Spore Archive

An independent archive dedicated to the long-term preservation of artworks, publications, research materials, and digital traces.

“Nothing survives alone.”

About

Nika Sandler's Spore Archive is an ongoing archival project focused on preserving artworks, publications, metadata, documentation, and related materials across multiple repositories.

The archive combines institutional infrastructures, decentralized storage, and independent preservation strategies. It aims not only to preserve artistic works but also to document the practice of digital archiving itself.

Preservation Principles

  • Multiple geographically distributed copies.
  • Use of open formats (TIFF, PNG, PDF/A, WARC, TXT, CSV).
  • Persistent identifiers (DOI).
  • Public accessibility.
  • Long-term documentation.
